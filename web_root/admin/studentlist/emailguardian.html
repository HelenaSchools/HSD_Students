Email Student Contacts


Click Email Guardians here after following setup below

ATTENTION OUTLOOK USERS:
PowerSchool stores guardian emails with COMMA separators.

To use this feature, you must set Outlook to ""Allow Comma as Address Separator""

HERE'S HOW

In Outlook, go to Tools>Options>Preferences>Email Options>Advanced Email Options

Select "Allow Comma as Address Separator" and click OK

Versions of Outlook may vary. If you have trouble, contact your building tech support.

REMEMBER:
Always send bulk parent emails BCC or blind carbon copy.

Select 100 or fewer recipients for each message. If the link does not work, you may need to select a smaller group size.

Email DOES NOT reach everyone.
Send sensitive or critical information through regular mail.
Use "Text Only" to copy and paste long address lists.
